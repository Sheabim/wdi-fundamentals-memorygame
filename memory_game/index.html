Intructions

Concentration, also know as Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkeisijaki, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped faceup over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching card.
